About Us: Learning About Sagada Together

Welcome to our website! We're here to help you discover Sagada, a super cool place in the Philippines.

Sagada

Sagada is one of the filming locations of the romantic comedy That Thing Called Tadhana. But the truth is, even before the movie came out, I had visited Sagada multiple times and noticed more and more people visiting each year. This increase in visitors happened because it was becoming easier to get there, tours were better organized, and more travelers were looking for new places to explore. So, Sagada was already on its way to becoming a well-known destination even before the movie made it even more famous. Sagada also holds a special place in our hearts for its unparalleled natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and the warmth of its people. I believe that every traveler deserves to experience the awe-inspiring landscapes, thrilling adventures, and profound sense of peace that Sagada offers. Let this be your guide to crafting the ultimate Sagada adventure. Discover insider tips on the best hiking trails, accommodations, and local cuisine to ensure your trip is nothing short of extraordinary. Whether you're a seasoned traveler or a first-time visitor, we'll help you create memories that will last a lifetime so lets delve into the depths of ancient caves, challenge yourself with exhilarating hikes, or immerse yourself in the vibrant traditions of the indigenous communities that call Sagada home.
