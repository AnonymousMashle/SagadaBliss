Sagada

Sagada is one of the filming locations of the romantic comedy That Thing Called Tadhana. But the truth is, even before the movie came out, I had visited Sagada multiple times and noticed more and more people visiting each year. This increase in visitors happened because it was becoming easier to get there, tours were better organized, and more travelers were looking for new places to explore. So, Sagada was already on its way to becoming a well-known destination even before the movie made it even more famous.

Sagada also holds a special place in our hearts for its unparalleled natural beauty, rich cultural heritage,
